DuckHunt's Arcade
------------------
A DuckHunt Creation
with help from LeeW

There are five small games of my devising
in this map, which all them cost points to
play.  This is how you "pay" to use this
arcade.  If you play well, you will win
back the points you paid, and then some.

Objective:
Play games to win points, or just for fun.

Cost/Reward:
Bowl-A-Rama  - 15/30
BreakOut     - 15/30
Pong(Ball)   - 10/10+
Pong(Player) -  5/10+
Whack-A-Mole - 20/40+
SlotMachine  -  1/100

Chance to win something at slots: 1/25
