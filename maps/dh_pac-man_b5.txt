DuckHunt's Arcade
Pac-Man Beta 5
------------------
A DuckHunt Creation

Objective:
Pac-Man - Eat all the power pellets.
Ghosts - Stop the Pac-Man.
Watchers - Watch and Laugh.

Scoring (Pac-Man):
-  1 team frags for each pellet.
-  5 team frags each power pellet.
- 25 team frags for eating all the pellets.
- 25 team frags for eating the bonus.
- 10 team frags for eating a ghost.

Scoring (Ghosts)
- 50 team frags for kiling Pac-Man
